Our Aim

At Little Minds Montessori

we strive to instill a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom, fostering a growth mindset that empowers children to navigate the complexities of tomorrow


Little Minds Montessori nurtures the whole child - intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically - laying the foundation for a lifetime of learning, growth, and success.

Guided by the principles of Maria Montessori

Our learning environment is thoughtfully designed to promote the following:

  • Practical Life

    Developing independence, self-care, and fine motor skills through everyday activities.

  • Sensorial

    Exploring and understanding the world through sensory experiences and hands-on discovery.

  • Mathematics

    Building foundational math concepts through manipulatives and real-world applications.

  • Language

    Culitvating reading, writing, and communcation skills through phonetics, literature, and creative expression.

  • Crafts & Creative Expression

    Encouraging imagination, creativity, and self-expresion through art, music, and movement.
